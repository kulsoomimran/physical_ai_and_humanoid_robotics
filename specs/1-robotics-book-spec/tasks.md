## Implementation Strategy

The project will follow an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) approach, delivering content incrementally. Each module, once its chapters are drafted and integrated, can be considered a deliverable increment for review and feedback.

**MVP Scope**: Module 1 - Foundations of Physical AI (Chapters 1-6).
